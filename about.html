You might be wondering who we are...

"Wamu" is an indigenous word from the Luganda language meaning "Together" and upon that basis, we aim at seeking all those out there with the potential to help the people in serious hardships to come join us on this journey of displaying the true meaninig of Humanity.

"For Ubuntu ya Maisha" This statement is deeply derived from the sweet words of the mother land and it reflects togetherness for Life. One can understand for Ubuntu ya Maisha as "The spirit of togetherness for life". Promoting, building, growing, developing greater lives of different individuals showing Unity and care for the development of those that might feel abandoned. But with Wamu Foundation, they are never abandoned!

Our Vision

A Uganda where every individual lives with dignity, hope and access to resources empowered by the spirit of togetherness.

Our Mission

To empower and uplift vulnerable groups of younglings and individuals in Uganda through online support from the citizens, fostering them into great people of the country.

Let it be you today to be the reason why this child can smile again.

This initiative was discovered by Mark Trevor Ssali a member of the Clansmen Group, an upcoming developmental brand and organization(of a young dedicated breed of gentlemen). He based it on his love for support and development of others, a common trait he shared with all the members of the Clansmen Group and together with his colleagues action was taken to see that the project comes to life.
This website was made by him for the people of Uganda with gorgeous kind hearts to forward a hand towards the children of Uganda and the disabled experiencing a difficult life so as to ensure that there is an end to that suffering they face and create smiles and real hope amongst them.

And if you're a Ugandan out there very passionate about supporting the development of suffering individuals, kindly join us today as we promote this forum for growth for all. Remember real development starts from the actions we take on as individuals and interconnectedness will always take us to greater heights. And so for that case ...

Wamu Foundation is an online Charity and Support Foundation founded to give a hand to the children in need and the disabled individuals and as well help them grow and develop in all ways possible to create a community of unity and equality among all individuals.
